1.2.5 Project - Super Fun Cup Game

This game creates three cups either blue or red depending on the user's color choise and randomly selscts one of the three cups to be special. The cups then shuffle and the user has two lives to guess which cup is special. When they guess wrong, one of the two hearts on the screen dissapears and once they lose both, they lose the game and it ends. If they guess the correct cup on the first or second try, then they win.
